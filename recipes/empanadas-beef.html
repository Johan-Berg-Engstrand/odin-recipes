Empanadas Beef Turnovers

Can serve as an appetizer, a main dish or even after your main course!

This beef empanada recipe is made with Goya Discos pastry rounds and a mouthwatering filling of seasoned ground beef, tomatoes, onions, and garlic.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Yellor or white Goya Discos empanadas discs, thawed

Steps

  1. Cook beef in a skillet over medium heat
  2. Add onions
  3. Add tomato sauce
  4. Put in empanada discs
  5. Put in mouth
